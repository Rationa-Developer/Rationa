🚀 Getting Started with Rationa

📝How to Log Your First Expense

1From the main screen, tap the blue "+ Log New Record" button

2Enter the amount you spent

3Add a description (e.g., "Coffee at Starbucks")

4Enter the location where you spent the money

5Set the date and time (defaults to now)

6Tap "Next" to select a category

7Choose the appropriate category (e.g., Food & Drinks, Transport)

8Tap "Save & Finish"

💡 Pro Tip: Record expenses immediately after making them - this helps you stay accurate and builds a good habit!

💳Understanding Your Budget

Your budget shows at the top of the main screen:

  • Total Budget: £4567.00 (your monthly limit)
  • Used Amount: £2313.00 (what you've spent)
  • Progress Bar: Blue bar shows 51% of budget used
  • Top Categories: Shows your biggest spending areas
💡 Budget Tip: When your progress bar turns red, you're approaching your budget limit!

💰How to Use Saving Plans

1Tap the "💰 Saving Plan" button at the bottom

2Tap "+ Add New Saving Plan"

3Set your goal (e.g., "Trip to Paris - Save £800")

4Add money to your plan by entering amounts in the "Add £..." field

5Track your progress with the percentage bar

6Add notes about where the money came from

🎯 Saving Tip: Set up automatic weekly savings! Even £20/week adds up to over £1000 per year.

📈Analyzing Your Spending Trends

1Tap "📊 Explore Trends" at the bottom

2Choose your time period: Daily, Weekly, Monthly, or Yearly

3View your spending graph to see patterns

4Use the date selector to compare different periods

5Switch between Bar Chart and Percentage views

⚠️ Watch for Spikes: If you see sudden spending increases, review what happened that month to avoid repeating it.

📝Using Notes for Financial Planning

1Tap "📝 Notes" to access your financial notes

2Tap "Add New Note" to create reminders

3Use tags to organize notes (e.g., #budget, #goals, #ideas)

4Search notes by tag using the search bar

💡 Note Ideas: Use notes to record financial goals, track irregular expenses, or remind yourself of spending rules.

📅 Daily/Weekly/Monthly Views

Switch between time periods using the tabs at the top:

  • Day View: See today's expenses with timestamps
  • Week View: Compare daily spending across the week
  • Month View: Track monthly totals and budget progress

Each view shows total spending for that period, helping you understand your patterns.

🔧 Troubleshooting Common Issues

Problem: Can't find a transaction

  • Check if you're in the right time view (Day/Week/Month)
  • Look for the transaction in a different category
  • Use the trash icon to check deleted items

Problem: Budget percentage seems wrong

  • Make sure you've set your correct monthly budget
  • Check that all transactions are in the right month
  • The percentage resets each month

Problem: Saving plan not updating

  • Make sure to tap "Save" after adding money
  • Check that you entered the amount correctly
  • Progress updates immediately after saving
