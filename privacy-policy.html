Privacy Policy for Rationa

Effective Date: [6 June 2025]

Last Updated: [6 June 2025]

Your Privacy Matters: Rationa is designed with privacy as a core principle. Your financial data stays on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address for account creation and support
  • Financial Data: Transaction records, budgets, and goals you enter (stored locally only)
  • Support Communications: Messages you send through our support system

Information Automatically Collected

  • App Usage Analytics: Basic usage statistics to improve the app (anonymized)
  • Crash Reports: Technical information to fix bugs (no personal data included)
  • Device Information: iOS version and device type for compatibility

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide Services: Enable core app functionality and features
  • Customer Support: Respond to your questions and provide assistance
  • App Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience
  • Security: Protect against fraud and abuse
  • Legal Compliance: Meet legal obligations when required

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

  • All your financial data is stored locally on your device
  • We never transmit your financial information to our servers
  • Data is encrypted using iOS built-in security features
  • You maintain full control over your financial data

Security Measures

  • Industry-standard encryption for any data transmission
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Minimal data collection principle
  • Secure authentication protocols

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • Service Providers: Trusted partners who help operate our service (with strict privacy agreements)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business Transfer: In the event of a merger or acquisition (with continued privacy protection)

Third-Party Services

Apple App Store

  • In-app purchases are processed by Apple
  • Apple's privacy policy applies to payment processing
  • We do not receive payment card information

Analytics Services

  • We may use anonymous analytics to improve the app
  • No personally identifiable information is shared
  • You can opt out of analytics in app settings

Your Rights and Choices

Access and Control

  • Data Access: Request information about data we have collected
  • Data Correction: Update or correct your account information
  • Data Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Data Export: Export your financial data from the app

Communication Preferences

  • Opt out of promotional emails
  • Control push notification settings
  • Manage in-app communication preferences

Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active
  • Financial Data: Stored locally on your device indefinitely
  • Support Communications: Retained for 2 years for quality assurance
  • Analytics Data: Aggregated data retained for service improvement

Children's Privacy

Rationa is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.

International Users

If you are using Rationa outside of [Your Country], please note that your information may be transferred to and processed in [Your Country] where our servers are located.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy in the app
  • Sending an email notification
  • Providing an in-app notification

Your continued use of Rationa after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

  • Email Rationa: rationa_developer@outlook.com
  • In-App: Use the "Contact Support" feature

Questions? We're committed to transparency about our privacy practices. Don't hesitate to reach out if you need clarification on any aspect of this policy.
