Privacy Policy for Rationa

Effective Date: December 2024

Last Updated: December 2024

Introduction

Welcome to Rationa ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Email Address: When you create an account
  • Financial Data: Budget amounts, expense records, categories, and notes that you input
  • App Preferences: Settings like dark mode, warning thresholds

Information We Don't Collect

  • We do NOT access your bank accounts, credit cards, or payment methods
  • We do NOT collect location data
  • We do NOT access your contacts or photos
  • We do NOT track your browsing habits outside the app

How We Store Your Information

Local Storage

  • All your financial data (expenses, budgets, notes) is stored locally on your device
  • We cannot access this data - it remains private to you
  • Data is not transmitted to our servers unless you explicitly choose premium features

Account Information

  • Your email address is stored securely to manage your account and premium features
  • We use industry-standard encryption to protect your email and account data

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain app functionality
  • Process premium membership purchases
  • Send important account and service updates
  • Provide customer support when requested
  • Improve our app based on user feedback

Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • App Store Payments: Apple processes premium purchases according to their privacy policy
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your account information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Request data portability

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Local data encryption on your device
  • Secure data transmission using HTTPS
  • Regular security updates and monitoring

Children's Privacy

Rationa is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: rationa.support@gmail.com
App: Use the "Contact Support" feature in the app

This policy is designed to be transparent about our data practices while protecting your privacy.
